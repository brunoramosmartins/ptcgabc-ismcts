# current-v1 (control)

Byte-for-byte copy of `decks/selected/deck.csv` (the Phase-0 Kaggle
sample deck). Included so every round-robin cell has the same format
and so the selection claim is anchored: **no candidate replaces v1
unless it beats this control convincingly.**

Profile: mono-Water; Snover → Mega Abomasnow ex core (Hammer-lanche:
discard top 6, 100× per {W} energy revealed — synergizes with the very
high energy count), Kyogre secondary, 35 {W} energy, thin trainer
suite (Mega Signal + Cyrano + shuffle-draw supporters), Maximum Belt
as the Ace Spec.

Known weaknesses: only 6 Basic Pokémon (mulligan-prone, thin bench);
35 energy means many dead draws once the board is set; no
switch/gust effects at all.
