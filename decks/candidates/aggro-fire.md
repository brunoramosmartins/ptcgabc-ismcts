# aggro-fire (big-basics, no evolution)

Hypothesis: with search agents that reward immediate value (F1/F2)
and no multi-turn combo planning, a deck of self-sufficient Basic
attackers wastes the fewest decisions. Zero evolution steps = zero
setup risk.

- 4× Gouging Fire ex (46): 260 dmg for {R}{R}{C}, 230 HP. Drawback
  (can't repeat Blaze Blitz until it leaves the Active Spot) is
  covered by 4 Switch + Latias.
- 4× Bloodmoon Ursaluna ex (44): all-colorless Blood Moon 240; the
  ability discounts it per opponent prize taken — natural late-game
  closer.
- 3× Mega Latias ex (754): 1-energy Strafe 40 + self-switch, retreat
  1 — the pivot. (Illusory Impulse is mostly dead without {P}; it's
  here for Strafe and 280 HP.)
- 4 Cyrano finds all three (all are ex). 4 Lacey + 3 Billy & O'Nare
  for draw, 2 Boss's Orders, 3 Pokémon Catcher (coin-flip gust),
  3 Potion, Prime Catcher as Ace Spec. 25 {R} energy.

11 Basics — the most mulligan-resistant of the candidates.

Risks: every attacker is a rule-box (2-3 prizes when KO'd) — losing
two Pokémon can lose the game; attack costs of 3 mean turn-3 at the
earliest without acceleration.
