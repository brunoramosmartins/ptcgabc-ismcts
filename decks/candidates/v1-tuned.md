are not. This candidate isolates that claim — same Snover → Mega
Abomasnow ex + Kyogre core, but:

- 35 → 27 {W} energy (still high on purpose: Hammer-lanche and
  Kyogre's Riptide both scale with {W} energy volume).
- +2 Bloodmoon Ursaluna ex (44): colorless late-game attacker whose
  Blood Moon gets cheaper as the opponent takes prizes.
- Trainer suite rebuilt: 4 Cyrano (search 3 ex — finds Abomasnow ex
  AND Ursaluna), 4 Mega Signal, 4 Lacey (shuffle-draw 4/8), 2 Boss's
  Orders (gust), 3 Switch, 3 Potion, Prime Catcher as Ace Spec
  (replacing Maximum Belt).

If v1-tuned beats current-v1 by a clear margin, the deck change is
justified even if the more aggressive archetypes disappoint.

Risks: 8 Basics is still modest; core attacker is a Stage 1 (setup
turn needed); Mega ex hands the opponent 3 prizes when KO'd.
